Did you know?

We all know water is important, but do you know why?

  • Water supports all the nutritive processes, from digestion and absorption, to utilization and excretion.
  • Water holds nutrients in solution and transports all food necessary for life to various parts of the body.
  • Water holds wastes and toxins it picks up from the cells and carries them to the organs of elimination.
  • Water is an essential constituent of all cells and tissues and of all body fluids.
  • Water keeps the various mucous membranes of the body soft and prevents friction between tissue surfaces.
  • Water is the main temperature regulator of the body, just like water in the radiator of the motor car.
