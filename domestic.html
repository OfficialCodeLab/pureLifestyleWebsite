Domestic

Not all water needs to be purified in the same way. We offer a range of purification solutions, focusing on your specific needs as well as the contaminants in your water. These range from basic Sediment and Carbon Filter Systems to Reverse Osmosis Purifiers and related Water Dispenser Products. Please contact us to assist you in making the correct decision.

2 Stage Filter Counter Top

Designed to be connected to a sink tap mixer, it removes sediments, taste, odour, chemicals, pesticides and herbicides. Plus, 50% of bacteria (this function is included in the Sediment and KDF/Carbon Filter).

2 Stage Filter Under Counter

It can be built up to a Reverse Osmosis Purifier and removes sediments, taste, odour, chemicals, pesticides and herbicides. Plus, 50% of Bacteria (This includes the Sediment and KDF/Carbon Filter with housings, stand and chrome faucet).

3 Stage Filter

It removes sediments, taste, odour, chemicals, pesticides and herbicides. Plus, 50% of bacteria (This includes one Sediment Filter, one Carbon Filter, one KDF/Carbon Filter, housings, stand and chrome faucet).

5 Stage Reverse Osmosis Purifier

There is a 5 year guarantee on the 5 stage system. It removes sediments, taste and odour, chemicals, pesticides, herbicides, 99% bacteria, 95% dissolved solids, all hormones and 94% radioactivity (This includes one Sediment Filter, two Carbon Filters, Reverse Osmosis Membrane, Post Taste Filter and a 10 liter storage tank)

Counter Top Distiller

It removes all sediments, taste, odour, chemicals, pesticides, herbicides, bacteria and dissolved solids, all hormones and radioactivity.

Counter Top Dispenser Units

It dispenses hot & cold purified water and can be connected to either a Filter or Purifier System.

Free Standing Dispenser Units

This dispenser supplies hot & cold water as well as water at room temperature. It can be connected to a Purifier/Filter system and is also available with a built-in Reverse Osmosis Purifier.

Office

Staff

  • What is the walking distance to the dispensing point/rest area?
  • How many floors does your staff occupy?
