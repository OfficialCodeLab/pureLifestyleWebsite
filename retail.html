Retail Products

We are a bunch of folk passionate about you and your customers. We exist to add value to you and your business, by creating refreshing, innovative solutions to bring “water to the people”, your customers, in a way that ”creates theatre” in your store and adds value to your bottom line.

Customers

The concept allows the Customer to:

  • Bring their own container
  • Purchase pre-filled bottles
  • Purchase a reliable, freshly produced, top quality product...
  • At a very reasonable price...
  • From an Attractive looking system

Dealers

The concept allows the Dealer to:

  • Sell a top quality product
  • At a very good profit margin
  • Off a small amount of floor space (1 square meter)
  • From a simply operated system
  • That creates theatre in store
  • Build Customer loyalty – the tank becomes the Customer’s source of water. Customer to “own” the tank.
  • Sell other products on the back of the water sale

It’s not just what we do that matters, but how we go about it. We sincerely believe that when doing business with you, we are here to build a relationship with you along the way.

Having pioneered the glass tank concept in 2002, we have realised the importance of keeping things simple, professional and relational with our customers. With our vision of “a tank in every town across the nations”, we have expanded our network of customers across South Africa, Namibia and into Botswana and Zambia.

Due to our pioneering spirit and relational approach, we have over three hundred and fifty systems installed in retail outlets across Southern Africa, working with Blue Chip companies, like Spar, Foodlover’s Market, Pick n Pay Family Stores, Ok Grocer, Total Bonjour and Engen forecourt stores, along with some independent retailers.

We would love for you to join us in expanding the concept of “bringing water to the people” and adding value to your store.
